About

Anonymous Forum For Transgender Students of Hollins
Here it is, I finally made it. This is a forum created by and for transgender students, a marginalized group in a very toxic school environment. Please use this to post trans-related information, questions, and to connect with other transgender students. Please contact the mediators and website creator with any comments, suggestions, or questions at: hellionsnetwork@gmail.com.

Guidelines

  • Each member of the forum should remain consistently anonymous, optionally using only their chatroom username, for everyone's safety. If you choose to create a username when posting or for the live chatroom, please make sure it is a username that will not easily identify you.
  • Everyone will have equal access to information provided on this forum. There will be no links to social media. This is a seperate entity.
  • All information provided, such as medical suggestions, should be sourced. This is to prevent any potential health risks.

Message from the website creator:

Hello and thank you for visiting my website. With the help of loved ones and the help of online resources, I've been able to provide a network for our transgender community here at Hollins and abroad. I created this website after coming to the conclusion that transgender students at Hollins not only need support and resources, but that love and information provided in a way that reduces the risk of anyone being outted. I vow to make this place as safe as I possibly can while each of us struggle every day to make our homes a safe space. This is where you have the ability to speak your mind, ask questions, provide information, and love to our transgender community. Every type of trans experience is welcome in this space. Every trans voice is welcome. Please contact me on the Live Chatroom (Slack) or by posting on either of the forums by addressing me as Creator and I will try to get back to you as soon as possible. Please use the live chatroom and the resources provided on this page for emergencies. I hope you enjoy your stay!
