Anonymous Forum For Transgender Students of Hollins
Here it is, I finally made it. This is a forum created by and for transgender students, a marginalized group in a very toxic school environment. Please use this to post trans-related information, questions, and to connect with other transgender students. Definition list.

Rules And Outlines

  • Each member of the forum should remain anonymous on the forum for everyone's safety. Members can create a username or stay entirely anonymous.
  • Everyone will have equal access to information provided on this forum. There will be no links to social media. This is a seperate entity.
  • All information provided, such as medical suggestions, should be sourced. This is to prevent any potential health risks.
